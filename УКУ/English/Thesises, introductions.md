# Homework
## Thesis
Giving homework is an art of balancing, because too much of it leads to either neglect or overstudying, too little to worse information consolidation, and not relevant one to loss of precious time

## Introduction
Homework is an essential part of studying. A lot of information is consumed during lessons, but to make it knowledge, personal efforts are required. When people learn something by themselves, they train what they need, but in the case of classes the responsibility lies on a teacher. And giving homework is an art of balancing, because too much of it leads to either neglect or overstudying, too little to worse information consolidation, and not relevant one to loss of precious time.
