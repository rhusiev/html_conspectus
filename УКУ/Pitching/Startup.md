# Startup vs business
*Growth - stability*

If you do *not* want to be *global, expand* - it's a *business*

Business plan - a huge document, good for business
For startup not so much, as nothing is stable
Better for *startup* - *business model - small, fast*

# Your journey
Be *passionate*

*Stress* will be 100%

*Friends* to support each other

# How to make
Idea - not necessarily about an innovation, but *a new point of view*

Investigate existing *customers*
About customers, not just the product. a good product without customers - bad
*Ask people*

So first - problem, then - solution

Mb make *mock product* before the solution *or even mvp* to show idea and get funding

# Get to know customers
*Not directly ask, if they want this product*
More like questions that lead to them coming up with their solution and view

*Better face to face*, at least *20* interviews to really understand

Listen to stories, *not interrupt* not very relevant ones - those can even lead to new ideas

*Note* everything - you can't remember everything

Better *not* ask *relatives, colleges* - they tend to not critique so much and be biased
