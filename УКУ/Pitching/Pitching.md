# General advice
Even in >20s pitches *the first few seconds* are the most important
If not interesting in the first 15 seconds, people lose attention

When pitching, there is not much time $\Rightarrow$ only tell *about what they might care*
*Simple*

*Logical structure*:
- Past -> present -> future
- Setup -> struggle -> happy end
- Problem -> solution -> benefit

Must end with a feeling *"tell me more"*

# Startup pitch
Investors like *structured* pitches:
1. What is the problem
2. My solution
They also like some *stories*:
- about a potential customer
- about how I made this
- about why this is important
- etc

Understand, *who you are talking to*: investor, possible clients...
