# Feedback to pitching school
## Bad
IceBreaker didn't really help, Tuesday's activities worked much better

Too little feedback for us in the 1 week

A lot of the same taught a couple of times(about startups, pitching). There are some new points made, but a lot of material is repeated

Invited guests were not aware what we already learned

They want from us a lot of feedback, but it is hard to give one. Many have feedback just to give feedback and get some points. It was really visible, as they oftentimes asked about things that were already discussed in the presentation and gave generic feedback that does not help the presenters. This requirement for feedback made the problem of its lack even worse, as now people didn't want to give a useful one, but a lot of it. On the other hand, I tried to give feedback only when I really saw a flaw in the presentation. But as I'm not very experienced, I didn't see as much, as teachers did. As a result, I talked rarer and had a lower mark

Too little time to change presentations(except last Thursday)
Generally for homework there was no time. After 8 hours of working, I was very tired and didn't want to do it. Started doing in the morning, before 10 AM, but requires standing up early

## Good
Better understanding, how to make presentations about myself, products. However, gained more experience in a long format(not elevator)

On the second day, when we were not doing a lot of work, we became friends(or at least got to know each other better and therefore felt more freely during the next presentations)

The most useful for me was the program of the second Tuesday about small talk, as it is hard for me now
